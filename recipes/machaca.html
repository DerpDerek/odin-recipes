Description

Mexican dried beef

Ingrediants

  • Pinto Beans 1 can
  • Diced Tomatoes 1 can
  • Onion 1 cup
  • Garlic 4 cloves
  • Oil 2 Tbsp
  • Tortillas
  • Cumin 2 tsp
  • Ground beef or Jackfruit
  • Eggs optional
  • Hot Sauce optional

Steps

  1. Dice Onions Finely
  2. In a bowl add beans
  3. Head pan or skillet with oil and onions. Cook until onions have softened and transulcent. Add spices, cumin or hot sauce
  4. Add jackfruit or ground beef and diced tomatoes
  5. Assemble ingredients on a tortilla
