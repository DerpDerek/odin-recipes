Description

Salad of the Western South

Ingrediants

  • Corn 1-16oz can
  • Black Beans 1- 16oz can
  • Cheddar Cheese 1/4 cup
  • Lettuce 1 1/2 cup
  • Green Onion 1/4 cup
  • Tomato 1/4 cup
  • Quinoa 1/4 cup
  • Limes 2 or Lemon 1
  • Olive Oil 1/3cup

Steps

  1. For 1 cup quinoa, add 2 cups of water and bring to a boil then let simmer for 15 minutes. Turn off heat and fluff grain and let sit covered for 5 minutes
  2. Mix juice of two limes with olive oil, salt and pepper
  3. Put all ingrediants ina bowl with lettuce as base, add toppings and citrus vinagrette
