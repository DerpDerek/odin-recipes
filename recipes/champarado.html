Description

Traditional Filipino Sweet Chocolate Rice Porrice

Ingrediants

  • Water 1.5 cups
  • Coconut Milk 1- 14oz can
  • salt 2 tsp
  • Glutinous Rice 1 cup
  • Chocolate/Cocoa Powder

Steps

  1. Boil Water
  2. Add chopped chocolate into a rice until fully incorporated
  3. Rinse Rice>
  4. Add coconut milk and reduce to simmer, add spices if you want
  5. Cook 15-20 minutes until creamy
  6. Let the rice steam and continue to thicken for 10 minutes
  7. Eat hot and serce with a drizzle of condensed milk
