Description

Curry and Noodles

Ingrediants

  • Onion 1 cup
  • Garlic 2 cloves
  • Spinach 2 cups
  • Carrots 2
  • Salt and Pepper to taste
  • Oil 2 Tbps
  • Noodles
  • Coconut Milk 1 can
  • Curry Powder 2 tbsp
  • Water
  • Ginger
  • Cilantro
  • Lime

Steps

  1. Cut onion in half and cut each half into thin slices. Do the same thing with carrots
  2. Mine garlic
  3. Heat oil in pot or pan
  4. Add onions until soften then add carrots for 3 minutes
  5. Add garlic and curry powder
  6. Add a can of coconut milk and instant noodles
  7. Simmer and cover for 10 minutes until fragrant and tender
  8. Add toppings
